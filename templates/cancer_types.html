Types of Cancer We Detect

Learn about the different types of cancer our system can identify

Brain Cancer

Overview

Brain cancer occurs when abnormal cells form in the brain tissue. It can be primary (originating in the brain) or secondary (spread from other parts of the body).

Common Symptoms

  • Headaches
  • Seizures
  • Memory problems
  • Balance issues
  • Vision changes

Detection Method

Our system analyzes MRI scans to detect abnormalities in brain tissue.

Breast Cancer

Overview

Breast cancer develops in the breast tissue, most commonly in the ducts or lobules. It's one of the most common types of cancer in women.

Common Symptoms

  • Lump in breast
  • Breast pain
  • Nipple discharge
  • Skin changes
  • Swelling

Detection Method

Our system analyzes mammograms to identify potential cancerous growths.

Lung Cancer

Overview

Lung cancer begins in the lungs and is often associated with smoking. It's one of the leading causes of cancer-related deaths worldwide.

Common Symptoms

  • Persistent cough
  • Shortness of breath
  • Chest pain
  • Weight loss
  • Fatigue

Detection Method

Our system analyzes chest X-rays and CT scans to identify lung abnormalities.

Prevention Tips

Avoid Smoking

Smoking is a major risk factor for many types of cancer, especially lung cancer.

Healthy Diet

Maintain a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains.

Regular Exercise

Stay physically active to maintain a healthy weight and boost immunity.

Regular Check-ups

Schedule regular medical check-ups and screenings.
