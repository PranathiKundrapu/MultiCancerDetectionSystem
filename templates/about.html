About Our Project

Advancing cancer detection through artificial intelligence

Our Mission

We are dedicated to making cancer detection more accessible and accurate through the power of artificial intelligence. Our system uses advanced deep learning models to analyze medical scans and provide quick, reliable results for various types of cancer.

Technology

Our system is built using state-of-the-art deep learning techniques, specifically the ResNet50 architecture, which has been fine-tuned for medical image analysis. The model has been trained on a diverse dataset of medical scans to ensure reliable detection across different cases.

Features

  • Real-time cancer detection
  • Support for multiple cancer types
  • High accuracy rates
  • User-friendly interface
  • Instant results
